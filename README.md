# Hospital-Performance-Dashboard-Patient-Insights-Analysis
The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of hospital performance by tracking key metrics such as total visits, patient satisfaction, and average waiting time, enabling better decision-making in healthcare management.

<h3><b> Key Features:</b></h3>

✔ KPI overview including Total Visits (4338), Average Satisfaction (5.03), and Average Wait Time (35.03 minutes)<br>
✔ Daily patient visit trends to monitor hospital traffic patterns<br>
✔ Patient distribution analysis by gender<br>
✔ Age group analysis to understand patient demographics<br>
✔ Patient race distribution insights<br>
✔ Department-wise referral analysis to identify high-demand services<br>
✔ Interactive filters for Year and Month selection<br>

<h3><b>Tools Used:</b><br></h3>
Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)<br>
Excel (Data preparation)<br>

 <h3><b>Outcome:</b><br></h3>
 
The dashboard helps hospital management monitor patient flow, improve service efficiency, and enhance patient satisfaction by identifying bottlenecks such as high wait times and heavily utilized departments.<br>

It enables data-driven decisions to optimize resource allocation and improve overall healthcare service delivery.
